Shipping Policy

At Lucky Scoopy, we are committed to delivering your orders as quickly and safely as possible.

Shipping & Delivery Time

Most orders are processed and dispatched within 24-48 hours of confirmation. You can expect your delivery within 3 to 5 business days across Pakistan.

Shipping Charges

We offer transparent shipping rates. The exact shipping fee is calculated at checkout based on your location. We frequently run free shipping promotions, so keep an eye on our store!

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation message. If you have any questions regarding your shipment status, please contact us at luckyscoop.pk11@gmail.com.

Delivery Areas

We currently ship all over Pakistan via our reliable courier partners. If you have any specific delivery instructions (like calling before arrival), please mention them in the order notes.
